# JINX-INTEL

JINX-INTEL is for intelligence fusion, incorporation, contextualization, and correlation.

It works with intelligence-derived information that is synthetic, open, summarized, declassified, or explicitly authorized. It does not collect intelligence, task collectors, expose source-sensitive details, or make final threat determinations.

## Phase 2 Behavior

- Represents synthetic or authorized intelligence summaries with reliability, restrictions, confidence, and provenance.
- Maps weather and communications context into human-review impact notices.
- Defaults unknown summaries to analyst review rather than inferred truth.
